DO A GAME MATE

Wanna play some blackjack? Cool, let's do this.

The rules are simple: you receive two cards, then you are given the choice of whether to stand (settle for your current score) or hit (draw another card). The aim of the game is to get a score as close to 21 as possible without going over. If your score is over 21 then you bust. If you get a total of 21, you win blackjack! Have fun!

When you're ready, click on deal and you will be dealt two cards!
