DO A GAME MATE

Wanna play some blackjack? Cool, let's do this.

The rules are simple: you start your hand with two cards, and after every draw you have the choice of whether to stick or twist. The aim of the game is to get a score as close to 21 as possible without going over. If your score is over 21 then you bust. If you get a total of 21, you win blackjack! Have fun!


Just click on the deck when you're ready and you will be dealt two cards!
